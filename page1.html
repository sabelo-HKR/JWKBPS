Matric Dance 2022

The Matric dance is way of saying farewell to the students as they finish high school and get ready for a new chapter in their lives. John wesley kosibay also had their matric dance to also farewell their students as it was the final year in high school. The matric dance was on the 30 july 2022, held at Thengane lodge West

The matric ball started at 18h00, but as usual, students decided that they will meet before that given time so they will be able to do convoy and show off around town as it was their big day. They all meet at Umhlabuyalingana stadium at 14h00. See photo below

After all students then went to the venue. On their way to the venue that when they got to do their convoy, even though it was dissaproved by the teachers. On their arrival to the venue they took pictures before starting the event.
