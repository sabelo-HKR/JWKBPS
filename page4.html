Valedictory

Valedictory is the annual premier event that marks the end of the Matric journey, where teachers get to bid a fond farewell in personalized speeches for matric students. Matric students also recieve trophy's and certificates for being top achiever throughout the year, for being student of the year, for well dressed uniform, for being a great company in class and etc. The following images are the students who received trophy's and certificates for their achievements in different categories of excellence!
