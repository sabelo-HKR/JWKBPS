Excursion

Matric 2022 had their trip to Isimangaliso wetland park in St.lucia as way to refresh their mind since they were to write their final examinations. The trip was on the 20th October 2022. ISimangaliso Wetland Park is a huge protected area along the coast of South Africa’s KwaZulu-Natal Province. The park’s centrepiece is the vast Lake St. Lucia, home to large numbers of hippos, crocodiles, pelicans and flamingos. Isimangaliso wetland park offers various activities which includes Game drive, boat cruise, look out view and etc.

Learners had their first activity where they were taken through crocodile species and further their knowledge about crocodiles, how they live and behave. After viewing the crocodiles their had their second activity of which is Game drive. They didn't see much animals as it was kinda late and animals by then were concealed.

They further their Excursion by hiking to the lookout point where they viewed the beauty of St.lucia landscape. This point has view of indian ocean and also the st.lucia as a whole. The pictures below dislpays the lookout point in st.lucia

After the lookout they went to beachfront resort in St.lucia Estuary for a view and after the beach front they went for a lunch. See images below!

After they had lunch, they went for a boat cruise. Boat cruise is water excursion where they are taught about hippopotumus species and get to witness other water animal species. In the water excursion they witness hippo families, snakes and fishies

The Boat cruise was their last activity in St.lucia and after it they went back to normal. Final examinations!
